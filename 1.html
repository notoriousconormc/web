conor

Conor Anthony McGregor (Irish: Conchúr Antóin Mac Gréagóir;[10] born 14 July 1988) is an Irish professional mixed martial artist. He is a former Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) featherweight and lightweight double-champion.[11] He is also the former Cage Warriors featherweight and lightweight champion. As of 19 July 2021, he is ranked #9 in the UFC lightweight rankings.
